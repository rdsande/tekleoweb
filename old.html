We want to make getting on the web easier for startups, small businesses and students.

TekleoWeb is a web hosting and design company based in Dar es salaam, Tanzania. We provide professional and affordable web hosting services custom made for Tanzanian creatives and small businesses. We want people to put their ideas and businesses online easily and cheaply.

We are also professional, experienced and affordable web developers in Tanzania. Our team is made up with creative ideas, sound strategy, attractive designs, effective content, and intuitive technology. We use our talents to deliver creative and productive web products and ultimately help businesses grow.
